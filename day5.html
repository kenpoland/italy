Scenic Gravel Cycling Route: Siena to Volterra

Route Overview

Start: B&B I Tetti di Siena, Via del Paradiso, 21, 53100 Siena, Italy

End: Villa Nencini, Borgo Santo Stefano 55, 56048 Volterra, Italy

This 55 km gravel route winds through the Tuscan countryside, featuring white roads (strade bianche), vineyards, and medieval villages.

Turn-by-Turn Directions

  • Depart from B&B I Tetti di Siena heading southeast on Via del Paradiso
  • Follow SP73 to Colle di Val d’Elsa
  • Turn onto SR68 toward Volterra
  • Pass through Mazzolla and continue to Borgo Santo Stefano
  • Arrive at Villa Nencini

Sights Along the Route

  • 🏰 Monteriggioni – Walled medieval village with panoramic views
  • 🌲 Chianti Sculpture Park – Outdoor art in nature
  • 🏛️ Roman Theatre of Volterra – Ancient ruins with mosaics
  • 🗿 Porta all'Arco – Etruscan gate dating back to the 4th century BC
