Gravel Cycling Adventure: Quercianella to Pisa

Route Overview

Start: Villa Tara, 11 Via don Italo Gambini, 57128 Quercianella, Italy

End: B&B Residenza d'epoca, Piazza Cairoli 12, 56127 Pisa, Italy

Distance: ~36.7 km

Terrain: Mixed gravel and paved roads with scenic coastal and countryside views

Turn-by-Turn Directions

  • Start at Villa Tara and head north on Via don Italo Gambini
  • Turn right onto Via del Littorale and follow the coast
  • Merge onto SP9 heading toward Livorno
  • Pass through Ardenza and Montenero
  • Continue on SP224 toward Collesalvetti
  • Turn left onto Via delle Sorgenti toward Pisa
  • Enter Pisa via Via Livornese
  • Arrive at Piazza Cairoli and B&B Residenza d'epoca
