Scenic Gravel Cycling Route: Quercianella to Pisa

Route Overview

Start: Villa Tara, 11 Via don Italo Gambini, 57128 Quercianella, Italy
End: B&B Residenza d'epoca Piazza Cairoli, Piazza Cairoli 12, 56127 Pisa, Italy

Turn-by-Turn Directions

  • Head north on Via don Italo Gambini toward Via della Pineta
  • Follow SP9 along the coast through scenic Castiglioncello
  • Turn inland at Rosignano Marittimo and follow gravel paths through the hills
  • Pass through Santa Luce and Orciano Pisano
  • Stop for lunch in Pieve di Santa Luce
  • Continue northwest through Fauglia and Collesalvetti
  • Enter Pisa via Via Livornese and head to Piazza Cairoli
