Scenic Gravel Cycling Route: San Gimignano → Siena

Route Overview

Start your journey at Donna Nobile Affittacamere, nestled in the medieval heart of San Gimignano. Follow the ancient Via Francigena through rolling Tuscan hills, vineyards, and olive groves to reach B&B I Tetti di Siena, a charming rooftop retreat in Siena.

Turn-by-Turn Directions

  • 🚴 Start at Donna Nobile, Via delle Romite 15, San Gimignano
  • Head southeast on Via Francigena gravel trail
  • Pass through Castel San Gimignano and enjoy panoramic views
  • Stop for lunch in Colle di Val d’Elsa
  • Continue south through Monteriggioni’s medieval gates
  • Final stretch into Siena via Strada di Pescaia
  • 🏁 Arrive at B&B I Tetti di Siena, Via del Paradiso 21
