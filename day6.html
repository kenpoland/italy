Scenic Gravel Cycling Route

From Villa Nencini, Volterra to Villa Tara, Quercianella

Turn-by-Turn Directions

  1. Start at Villa Nencini, Borgo Santo Stefano 55, Volterra
  2. Head southeast on SP15 toward Saline di Volterra
  3. Turn left onto SP68 and follow signs to Cecina
  4. Merge onto SP39 toward Rosignano Marittimo
  5. Continue south on Via del Littorale to Quercianella
  6. Arrive at Villa Tara, Via don Italo Gambini 11
