Scenic Gravel Cycling Route: Volterra to Quercianella

Route Overview

Start your journey at Villa Nencini, Borgo Santo Stefano 55, 56048 Volterra, Italy, and cycle through the rolling Tuscan hills to B&B Villa Tara, Via don Italo Gambini 11, 57128 Quercianella, Italy. This scenic gravel route covers approximately 85 km and offers breathtaking views, cultural landmarks, and delicious local cuisine.

Turn-by-Turn Directions

  • Depart Villa Nencini heading southeast on SP15.
  • Pass through Roncolla and Ulignano, enjoying views of olive groves and vineyards.
  • Continue toward San Gimignano for a mid-route break.
  • Take SP68 toward Cecina, then follow coastal roads south to Quercianella.
  • Arrive at B&B Villa Tara.
